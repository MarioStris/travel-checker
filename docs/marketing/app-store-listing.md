## Description (4000 znakova)

```
Your world, mapped.

Travel Checker is the iOS app for people who take travel seriously —
not just the Instagram moments, but the real costs, the details, and
the memories that deserve to last more than 24 hours.

---

WHAT MAKES TRAVEL CHECKER DIFFERENT

Most travel apps do one thing: track countries, or plan trips, or store
photos. Travel Checker does all three — and adds the one thing nobody else
has: real budget tracking per destination.

You'll finally know:
• How much your week in Lisbon actually cost
• Which destinations give you the best value for money
• How your total travel spending breaks down across years

---

YOUR WORLD MAP

Every trip you add becomes a pin on your interactive world map. Tap a
pin and see photos, costs, and details. Zoom out and see your entire
travel life at a glance.

47 countries? 12 cities? 3 continents this year? Your map knows.
And it grows with every new adventure.

---

REAL BUDGET TRACKING

This isn't a spreadsheet. Enter your costs by category — accommodation,
food, transport, activities, and other — and Travel Checker does the math.

Average cost per trip. Total travel budget. Cost per day. Per destination.
All calculated automatically.

Because knowing how much you actually spend is the first step to traveling
more for less.

---

YOUR TRAVEL JOURNAL

Add photos (up to 10 per trip), a detailed description, accommodation
details with links, and exact dates. Everything in one place, not scattered
across apps, camera rolls, and bank statements.

10 years from now, you'll be glad you wrote it down.

---

TRAVELER CATEGORIES

Are you a solo explorer? A digital nomad? A family traveler? A backpacker
chasing $20 hostel beds? A luxury traveler who knows what they want?

Select your traveler type and get an experience tailored to how you actually
travel. Because a digital nomad's priorities are very different from a
family of four.

10 categories: Solo, Couple, Family, Backpacker, Luxury, Digital Nomad,
Adventure, Cultural, Group, Business.

---

FEATURES

• Interactive world map with custom pins for every destination
• Tap any pin to see trip details, photos and budget breakdown
• Budget tracking by category: accommodation, food, transport,
  activities, other
• Photo gallery — up to 10 photos per trip
• Trip statistics: countries, cities, continents, total budget
• Average budget per trip and per day
• Google Places autocomplete for destination search
• Season tracking (spring/summer/autumn/winter + exact dates)
• Accommodation details with link and rating
• Public or private trips — you decide what the world sees
• User profile with travel statistics
• 10 traveler categories for personalized experience

---

COMING SOON

• Share your map and trips to Instagram, WhatsApp, and more
• Social feed — see trips from travelers you follow
• Advanced statistics and insights
• Unlimited photos (premium)

---

PRIVACY & DATA

Your private trips are visible to you alone. We use industry-standard
encryption for all data. We don't sell your data. We don't show ads.

Full privacy policy: travelchecker.app/privacy

---

ABOUT US

Travel Checker is built by travelers, for travelers. We were frustrated
that no app combined a beautiful map with honest budget tracking —
so we built it.

We're a small team and we read every review. If you have feedback,
suggestions, or find a bug: support@travelchecker.app

Your world is waiting. Start mapping it.
```

## Ratings & Reviews Strategy

### Prompt za Review (in-app)

**Okidač:** Nakon što korisnik doda 3. putovanje (milestone)
**Timing:** Minimum 7 dana od instalacije
**Copy:**
```
Enjoying Travel Checker?

If you're finding it useful, a review on the App Store means the world to us —
and helps other travelers find the app.

[Not Now]  [Rate App ⭐]
```

**Napomena:** Koristiti SKStoreReviewAPI, ne custom prompt. Apple policy zahtijeva standardni API.

### Odgovaranje na Reviews

**Na svaki 1-2 zvjezdice (unutar 24h):**
```
Thanks for taking the time to leave a review — even critical ones help us improve.
We'd love to understand what went wrong. Please reach out: support@travelchecker.app
and we'll make it right.
```

**Na 5 zvjezdica:**
```
This makes our day! Thanks for taking the time.
If you have friends who travel and struggle to keep track of trips and costs —
share the app with them. Every new traveler on the map makes it better for everyone.
```

## Competitive Positioning u App Storeu

### Search Ads Strategy (Apple Search Ads)

**Prioritetni keywordi za kampanje:**
- "trip tracker" — direktna intencija
- "travel journal app" — visok volume, relevantno
- "travel budget tracker" — naš differentiator, niska konkurencija u plaćenoj
- "countries visited" — specifičan intent
- "polarsteps" (competitor) — allowed u Search Ads, ne u organic keywords

**Budget preporuka (prvih 30 dana):** 500-1000 EUR za testiranje, optimizirati na temelju CPI podataka.

**Target CPI (Cost Per Install):** < 1.50 EUR (industry prosjek za travel apps je 1.80-2.50 EUR, freemium pomaže)
