## Title

Character count: 29 / 30

**Rationale:** Google Play title gets more weight in Play Store search than Apple's App Name.
"Trip Journal" is the highest-volume relevant keyword combination for this category on Android.

## Review Strategy (Android)

**In-app review trigger (Google Play In-App Review API):**
- Trigger after user adds their 3rd trip (same logic as iOS)
- Use Play Core In-App Review API (does not require leaving the app)
- Do NOT trigger more than once per 30 days regardless of user action
- Do NOT gate the review prompt behind "rate us" CTA buttons

**Response strategy:**
- Respond to all reviews within 48 hours (Play Console allows developer replies)
- Negative reviews: empathetic reply + direct support contact within 24 hours
- Positive reviews: personalized thanks (not copy-paste)
- Tag reviews by theme monthly to identify top feature requests
